Chase Game

Game Rules

You are the survivor. Run from the killer, punch to stun them, dash away with E, and heal with Q only after landing a punch.

Controls

  • Move: WASD / Arrow Keys
  • [R] Punch: Stun the killer if close enough
  • [E] Dash: Quick burst of speed
  • [Q] Heal: Only works after landing a punch
